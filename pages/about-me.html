Hi I'm Ria! I am a software engineer based in Seattle currently working on the Commercial Software Engineering Team at Microsoft working with Microsoft's most valuable customers on new technology and perfecting engineering process.

I grew up right outside of Chicago, and I spent the last four years in New York where I graduated from Columbia University. I studied computer science while I was there, but also loved taking classes in film and art history.

Recently, I have enjoyed finding ways to explore the intersection of art, storytelling, philosophy, and technology. Particularly, data visualizations have been a fun way I've been telling some fun stories about pop culture. Explore my work to see how I've been doing this and/or reach out to me to discuss further!
